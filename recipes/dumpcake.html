Dump Cake

This eggnog dump cake could not be easier! It uses the classic 3-ingredient formula for dump cakesâ€”perfect for popping in the oven at a moment's notice! Cake mix, eggnog, and butter are all you need. Serve with whipped topping or ice cream for the perfect finish.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups eggnog
  • 1 box white or yellow cake mix
  • 1/2 cup butter, cut into small pieces
  • whipped topping, for serving (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Pour 1/3 of the eggnog into the prepared dish. Sprinkle cake mix evenly over eggnog. Pour remaining eggnog evenly over the top of cake mix. No need to stir!
  3. Distribute pieces of butter evenly on top.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and bubbly, 45 to 50 minutes.
  5. Allow dump cake to cool for 15 minutes before serving to allow the cake to set slightly. Serve with cool whip, if desired.
